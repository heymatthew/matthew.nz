Reflections on Retros

Without care the Scrum retrospective format becomes ineffective. Hallmarks include actions without owners, avoidance of difficult conversations, and productivity stagnation. If this is happening and you’re able to take proactive steps to adjust the team environment then your role has changed.

Initially the retrospective format supports you, but as you grow you need to step up to take more responsibility to support the process. Teams where I had experienced people who lifted people up had great retrospectives. Pay it forward and support the process to lift others.

If your role changes your goal should be to listen, suggesting action, give gratitude, create safe spaces for dialogue, and take ownership for outcomes.
