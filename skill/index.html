Thinking about my own adaptability my knife skills are ok but when I’m making a meal I often have to look up temperature, timings, and measurements. If I meet a chef or food scientist I might use my use stories to socialise, and thinking I have some good skill with the knife I may share my principles with a master. They become bored fast. Our skills do not match, the conversation moves on.

In the modern world we have to work with people at different experience levels even across disciplines. If you’re working with someone at any skill level, be curious about their perspective. If you recognise you’re dancing with someone who has more skill, let them lead the conversation more.

AI is like driving an autonomous vehicle. You can’t take your eyes off the road, but when you need to take over it’s at the worst possible moment.

Super Car vs Model S, the former puts you in control but the latter you’re babysitting.
