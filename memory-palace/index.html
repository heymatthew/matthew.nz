CURE

Create memory palaces

  • You have to make a few to get good at it. Build them.
  • Optimise by doing small Thins
  • Then you get the most out of your spacial memory

Use your memory palaces

  • You haven’t broken it in if you don’t use it
  • Be there with your palace to remember it
  • Results. You can’t manage what you can’t measure, if you can’t measure it you can’t improve it.
  • Sketchbooks let you look back at your past, see the territory covered, you can use this to look forward to your future

Rack em and stack em

  • Striking visualistaions
  • Connect with what you already know
  • Use movement/action– action and reaction
  • Draw your magnetic imagery and think of where it is
  • Mind mapping with memory palaces are great

Explain to your brain

  • Recall reversal. Go through a few orders and patterns to get past the forgetting curve
  • Using serial positioning is an alternative to spaced repetition
  • Explain to your brain what is there
  • Language learning, speak, write, listen as much as possible
